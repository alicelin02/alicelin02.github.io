For all the important events in life now you have the chance to book multiple tables at once! Wether you're looking for a place to have a laid-back birthday celebration, organising a family reunion, or need a place for a committee meeting, we've got you covered!

We make custom cakes, so every birthday can be perfect. We also take scone and pastry orders if you have a specific treat in mind.

We can offer some separation from the general public, so you won't be disturbed by the outside world!
